Studying Made Eiser! Bojonegoro Regrency Government Offers Scholarships for Students
Audina Hutama | Activity | scholarship, Bojonegoro, Unigoro

BOJONEGORO - The Bojonegoro Regency Government is once again offering scholarships to residents and students with Bojonegoro National Identification Numbers (NIK). Three types of scholarships are being offered: Islamic boarding school scholarships, scholarships for low-income families, and final project scholarships. Bojonegoro University (Unigoro) is the private university (PTS) that accommodates the recipients of these Bojonegoro Regency Government scholarships.
